Functions are now partial (not total). Furthermore, recursive types range
over computations, so the recursion is not done immediately. 

\subsection{Syntax}

\begin{synchart}{}
  \TypeSort     & \tau & \bnfdef & \ldots                     & \ldots                     & \text{everything else} \\
                &      & \bnfalt & \parrtyabt{\tau_1}{\tau_2} & \parrtycst{\tau_1}{\tau_2} & \text{partial function} \\
  \\
  \ExprSort     & e    & \bnfdef & \ldots                     & \ldots                     & \text{everything else} \\
                &      & \bnfalt & \genrecabt{\tau}{x}{e}     & \genreccst{\tau}{x}{e}     & \text{recursion}
\end{synchart}
Everything else does not include inductive/coinductive types.
