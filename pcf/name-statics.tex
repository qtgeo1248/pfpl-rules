\subsection{Statics}

Most of the statics are the same, except lambda functions can be partial:

\boxed{\typeJ{\Gamma}{e}{\tau}}
\begin{mathpar}
  
\Infer
  {\typeJ{\Gamma, \IsOf{x}{\tau_1}}{e}{\tau_2}}
  {\typeJ{\Gamma}{\lamcst{\tau}{x}{e}}{\parrtycst{\tau_1}{\tau_2}}}

\Infer
  {\typeJ{\Gamma}{f}{\parrtycst{\tau_1}{\tau_2}} \\ \typeJ{\Gamma}{e}{\tau_1}}
  {\typeJ{\Gamma}{\appabt{f}{e}}{\tau_2}}

\Infer
  {\typeJ{\Gamma, \IsOf{x}{\tau}}{e}{\tau}}
  {\typeJ{\Gamma}{\genrecabt{\tau}{x}{e}}{\tau}}

\end{mathpar}
