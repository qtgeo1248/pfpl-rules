In this case, only functions can be recursive. No more $\genrecabt{\tau}{x}{e}$.

\subsection{Syntax}

\begin{synchart}{e}
  \TypeSort     & \tau & \bnfdef & \ldots                           & \ldots                           & \text{everything else} \\
                &      & \bnfalt & \parrtyabt{\tau_1}{\tau_2}       & \parrtycst{\tau_1}{\tau_2}       & \text{partial function} \\
  \\
  \ExprSort     & e    & \bnfdef & \ldots                           & \ldots                           & \text{everything else} \\
                &      & \bnfalt & \funabt{\tau_1}{\tau_2}{f}{x}{e} & \funcst{\tau_1}{\tau_2}{f}{x}{e} & \text{recursive function}
\end{synchart}
As before, this does not include inductive/coinductive types.
